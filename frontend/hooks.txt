TCP Controller Documentation
Load/Save System
NEW.Character - Creates a new character and resets all values back to default (Lucy).
NAME.Lucy - Replace Lucy with your name.
BTN.Save - Saves your customization options.
Load.Lucy - Replace Lucy with your desired agent’s name. This loads a specific agent customization save.
Delete.Lucy - This deletes a specified agent customization save. Replace Lucy with your desired agent’s name.
